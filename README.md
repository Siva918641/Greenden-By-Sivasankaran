GreenDen - Online Plant Shop
Overview

GreenDen is a modern online plant shop that allows users to explore, learn about, and purchase a variety of plants. The platform provides both online ordering and in-store experiences, ensuring high-quality plants with helpful guidance for beginners and plant enthusiasts alike.

The website features:

Responsive design with HTML, CSS, and Tailwind CSS

Product showcase with best-sellers and rare plants

Customer reviews section

Contact form for queries and gardening guidance

Features

Responsive Layout: Works seamlessly across devices

Product Section: Highlights popular and rare plants with pricing

Customer Reviews: Display real testimonials from users

Newsletter Signup: Stay updated with offers, plant tips, and news

Contact Form: Allows users to reach out for guidance and support
